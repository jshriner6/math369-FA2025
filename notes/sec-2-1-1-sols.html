    3. Definition: Linear Combination.

    The linear combination of the vectors \(\vvec_1, \vvec_2, \dots, \vvec_n\) with scalars \(c_1, c_2, \dots, c_n\) is the vector
    ðŸ”—
    Solution.
    \begin{equation*} c_1\vvec_1 + c_2\vvec_2 + \ldots + c_n\vvec_n\text{.} \end{equation*}
    The scalars \(c_1,c_2,\ldots,c_n\) are called the weights of the linear combination.
